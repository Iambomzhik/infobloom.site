They called me from my same number to offer me an Invex Volaris card

ByInfobloom

May 24, 2022

Sections of this article:

This morning I received two very strange calls, they were from my cell phone number. Obviously, everything pointed to being a fraud, because he did not know how this was possible. I answered out of curiosity and the first time a woman answered, but the audio or the signal was very low, so it was cut off a lot. In addition, it sounded as if the lady was on the street or in a crowded place, there was a lot of noise, but it did not sound like a Call Center normally does. I hung up.

A few hours later the same thing happened again, they called my cell phone and the call came from my own phone number. I answered to see if I got any response. This time a guy who knew my name answered and said he was from a bank I’ve never had a relationship with.

The reason for the call, said the man on the other end of the line, was to offer me a card Invex Volaris, with discounts on flights and other things that they usually say to get you hooked. I told him I wasn’t interested and asked him why he was dialing my same number, but there was no answer and he hung up.

Why did they call me from my same number? This says the police

After this crazy situation, I went to Google to look for an answer and the first thing I found was a video of the former Federal Police and its cyber security area, in which they say that ‘you should not be scared’ and point out the following:

“As a result of our investigations, it has been identified that it is linked to the commercial activity used by some telemarketing systems to offer products and services. The objective of this technique is to motivate the user’s curiosity to answer the call.

Ok, if the Federal Police says it’s something normal, the ‘scare’ gets over you, you take it as an anecdote, tell it a couple of times and forget it. But the video does not end there, the agents indicate that they have no reports of scams or theft, in addition to the fact that there is no way to steal your data through these calls.

They add that they work with the companies to help mitigate these “nuisance” calls and simply recommend that Mexicans not answer the phone when they are called from the same number.

So, the Federal Government cyber police did not know about this technique and they had to do research to deduce that? No other institution knew about it? And how does this practice work? How can companies trick telephone operators into making calls from a person’s number? Is this legit? And if someone in need of support falls for one of these calls that does turn out to be a fraud and all their data is stolen, then will they take it as a crime?

Invex Volaris card calls

Although the now defunct Federal Police says that ‘you shouldn’t be scared’, the answer didn’t convince me and I looked for the product they offered when they called me from my same number: the card Invex Volaris.

It turns out that the bank Invex yes it exists. Although seeing the comments on their social networks made by their clients, they do not seem to have the best service in the world. The cards Invex Volaris they are also real and are offered on their website.

Now, there are pages on the Internet to search for phone numbers that make scam calls or that offer bank services, cards or promotions, even when you are not a customer.

Yes, it is a fraud, admits Volaris… and the police

Searching on those websites I found that the Invex Volaris card It has been offered under this modality since 2013! In other words, people have been receiving calls from the same number for seven years and it took the Federal Police at least four years to realize it and do their investigations.

In 2017, when the Federal Police released their video to tell you not to be scared by this situation, the calls of this type grew. But in that same year, several users asked, via Twitter, Volaris about whether the card offer was real.

Volaris answered yes, the Invex card It already existed at that time, but it is by no means offered in that form. So what if it is a crime? Weeks before this, the Federal police corrected herself and tweeted to say that the calls made from your same number are a scam.

recommendations

The most obvious, do not answer

It is true that not answering a strange call or made from your same number is for now the best way to avoid being scammed, although you can also take other preventive measures.

Don’t give your number to anyone

The buying and selling of personal data, including phone numbers and emails, is a practice that has existed between product and service companies for years.

The affected party is the client, because they do not know where the data they entrust to a particular company will end up, so it is better to be jealous of our data and not provide it to anyone, only to the most necessary.

Example, yes it is good that your bank or have your phone, but you are not going to give it to a guy who offers free samples of a yogurth in the Alameda, in exchange for you filling out a form with all your information.

Search for the number in Google and help forums

For example, before this situation I received constant calls from foreign numbers or unknown to me, most of them were to offer credit cards Banorte, Santander either American Express.

Before answering, I searched the internet for the number and results appeared saying it was a scam, I hung up the phone and blocked the number, most Smartphones now allow this.

Then I contributed to these forums (one of the most reliable is www.quienhabla.mx) providing the details of the call: the number, the entity where I live, the day they called me, the service they claim to offer and a warning. You don’t have your personal data, not even your name or email, so no problem.

Register in the REPEP

Another option is to sign up for the Public Registry to Avoid Publicity, which is an official tool of the prophet so that companies cannot call you.

You can register your number on the official page of the prophet (click here) or in the following numbers:

  • Mexico City, Guadalajara and Monterrey. 96 28 00 00
  • Interior of the Mexican Republic at 800 962 8000.

Report nuisance calls and scams

It is difficult to report a call made from your same number because there is no way to know who did it, but it is best to alert the police about the situation so that they give more attention to this problem.

The official number to report this type of nuisance calls or scams is 088 for the entire country and operates 24 hours a day.

Now that you know more about the subject, don’t reply to calls from your same numberdenounce them and do not accept a Invex Volaris card by this means, since they will surely steal your data and even your money.
