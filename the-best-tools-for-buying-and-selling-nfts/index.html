The best tools for buying and selling NFTs

The correct tools can provide powerful insight to guide your investment strategy, and as with DeFi and the trading In general, this also applies to NFT’s. We have thoroughly researched the subject and present you with a list of best NFT tools to help you choose the best NFTs.

In this article, we cover some of the Top tools, resources and markets to help you discover new NFTs and make it easy to find your digital collection.

1. Rarity.Tools

Rarity Tools is a platform that ranks generative art and collectible NFTs based on their rarity. With this information, NFT collectors can more easily value and compare the relative value of individual NFTs to one another. You can also check out upcoming NFT releases on Rarity Tools.

2.Icy.Tools

Icy Tools is an NFT tracking platform where you can see the latest NFT base prices, volume and sales history. You can also discover new projects that are being minted and follow portfolios. Plus, it has helpful discovery features like the ability to see which collections are trending, 7-day sales volume, and floor prices.

3. NiftyRiver

niftyriver is an NFT site to find the latest collections, releases, and rarity rankings of popular new projects on Ethereum, Solana, and Wax. Although less popular than Icy Tools and Rarity Tools, some NFTs are classified here and not on the above platforms.

4.NFT Stats

NFT Stats allows you to follow the activity in the NFT markets and discover the best selling NFT items and collections. You can track which NFTs are selling and which are not, and use it to get the latest data on the NFT market by day, week, and month.

5. wgmi.io

wgmi (We’re Gonna Make It!) is an NFT price floor tracking and account valuation tool. It tracks NFT base prices and transaction volume over various time periods.

6.NFT Price Floor

NFT Price Floor is an NFT data aggregator that tracks base prices, trading and selling volumes, and more across multiple collections.

7.Trait Sniper

Trait Sniper is an NFT metadata tracking tool that gives you a full rarity rating within 30 seconds of reveal. For subscribers, there are also exclusive features like quick checkout, access to a reveal alert bot, and more. The free version of the tool is a rarity checking platform where newly revealed collections are made available to the public after 1 hour.

8.dAppRadar

dAppRadar allows you to discover the hottest NFT collections, market rankings and top sales in real time. As a discovery platform, it allows you to visualize the main collections in multiple time frames. dAppRadar supports NFTs from multiple blockchains such as Ethereum, Solana, Wax, and FLOW.

9. NFT Gas Station / Pump My Gas

NFT Gas Station is a website that helps you compare the current ETH gas prices on the major Ethereum NFT platforms.

10.PartyBid

PartyBid is a product that allows groups of people to combine their capital and bid together in NFT auctions. Anyone can create or join a PartyBid, contribute ETH, and place bids on supported platforms like Zora, Foundations, Opensea, and Nouns as part of a collective.

11. Check My NFT

Check My NFT is a metadata checking tool to check how well your ERC-721 NFTs secure their metadata. By entering the NFT contract address and token ID, you can see how robust your NFT assets are: whether they are immutably stored in on-chain storage, or stored in a centralized provider like Google Drive.

12. SudoSwap

sudoSwap allows NFT traders to carry out optimized P2P trades of any basket of ERC-20, ERC-721 or ERC-1155 tokens with ease. It acts as a decentralized market for NFTs and your assets do not leave your wallet unless the exchange is accepted by both parties.

13.NFT Trader

NFT Trader is a peer-to-peer NFT OTC trading protocol that allows traders to trade ERC-721, ERC-1155 or ERC-20 NFTs safely and easily. To do this, it uses a smart contract that acts as a trusted intermediary for exchanges, reducing the risks of fraud.

14. Flawn

Flawnt allows you to curate, share, and remix your digital asset collections through a lightweight platform. It is a platform that allows you to enjoy a slideshow presentation of your assets in a minimal way.

15.Showtime

Showtime is a social media platform for NFTs where both creators and collectors can showcase their digital art. You can think of it as an Instagram for NFTs where you can like, comment, follow and discover new profiles.

16.Opensea

opensea is the world’s first and largest NFT marketplace where you can buy, sell and discover exclusive digital items.

17.Foundation

Foundation is a platform that aims to build a new creative economy. It bridges the gap between cryptocurrencies and culture to foster a network of mutual support between artists, creators, and collectors.

18. Mintable

mintable is a digital item marketplace where you can create, buy and sell digital items powered by the blockchain. You can also use Mintable to mint and manage NFTs on the blockchain.

19. Studio 721

Studio 721 is a free, all-in-one tool for configuring, deploying, and verifying custom NFT smart contracts. Supports key features used by PFP and generative art collections, such as multimint, token parameters, revenue splitting, token controlled access, and more

20.Magic Eden

magic eden is a market for NFTs in Solana. Its goal is to democratize access to NFTs for artists and collectors by building an immersive cross-chain NFT marketplace, leveraging the power of Solana and Serum to become the home of the next generation of digital creators.
