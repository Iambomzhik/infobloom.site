Hacking and Spying WhatsApp with Phone Number Is it Possible?

ByInfobloom

May 29, 2022

Are you looking for how to spy or hack WhatsApp for free and safely? Be careful, many apps, software and websites try to scam and trick users into believing that they can pull off this criminal feat.

But what leads a person to want to spy on conversations of WhatsApp? Let’s face it, most people looking for this want snoop on the chats of your partner, girlfriend or husband. The reason? Although we are not going to talk about these issues in this technology blogwe assume that it is due to jealousy or suspicions of infidelity.

Whatever the reason, these people come to Internet mainly to find out how they can achieve this, without even knowing the dangers they are exposing themselves to and the consequences that this could cause them.

Safety pin: 41 WhatsApp tricks you didn’t know

In this article we will show you how some of the main web pages and applications work that ensure you can hack anyone’s WhatsApp in a matter of secondswith only the telephone number of the “victim”.

Pages to Hack and Spy on WhatsApp

There is a proliferation of scam websites of all kinds on the Internet that try to trick users in order to obtain money. And the scams about the most popular instant messaging application are no exception.

When conducting searches on the Internet (mainly Google) about how to spy or hack whatsapp with mobile numberin the first results appear these malicious websites that deceive users into believing that they will be able to spy on a person’s WhatsApp without them noticing.

Of all these sites, here we show you how the most popular websites work, such as Hackingtor WhatsApp, and in which many unwary people fall:

Spy WhatsApp

The most popular and the one that ranks best in search engines is this unethical website to “spy on WhatsApp” (espiarwapp.com).

Upon entering the web, we find a “Spy now” button. Clicking on it shows us a window to enter the phone number. The country of the cell phone number does not matter (Mexico, Argentina, Spain, etc.), because “the service is international”.

After entering the number and clicking the button, the process of the alleged “hack” will start. Of course, this does nothingjust making you waste your time.

When finished, it takes us to a page with the supposed information that it obtained from the entered number. Here you will see information on the number of conversations, images, videos, voice notes, total file size, the WhatsApp HASH and the date of the attack.

Obviously, these data are false. In fact, whatever number we enter, it will always show this same data.

It also shows us two “private servers” to access WhatsApp Web. This to supposedly read the WhatsApp messages of the other mobile.

This is where unsuspecting users fall for this scam and end up clicking on these buttons. These links do not lead to any server, but to advertising pages to install some browser extension or to subscribe to some premium service.

Example of an advertising page to which the links to spy on WhatsApp take you.

Here, do not enter your card details for any reason, since you will be subscribed to a premium service.

hack whatsapp

Another misleading website that promises hack a whatsapp number in a matter of seconds is Hackear.co. On this website they even show alleged images of people with “hacked WhatsApp”.

The scam is the same. After entering a cell phone number (valid or not), the web begins to “work” showing the supposed requests to the server, brute force attacks to hack WhatsApp, exploiting vulnerabilities in the database, until finishing with the “hacking” .

At the end, it shows us the supposed hacked WhatsApp data. False information from chats, photos, videos, audios, documents and number of contacts.

Unlike the previous service, this data is random, to try to confuse the user and thus end up falling for the scam.

To make it more credible, they post false user reviews attesting that the service works.

The deception lies in the “Download data” button. If you click on this button you will be redirected to a advertising website where they request to register to be subscribed to a multimedia service (movies, music, books, etc.)

Again, let’s avoid falling for these scams believing that we will be able to hack WhatsApp online for free without a credit card and without surveys.

hacker whatsapp

The popular website to spy on WhatsApp with Hackingtor could not be missing. A page that offers a supposed free “service” that works with any phone number.

Upon entering hackingtor.co we find the typical WhatsApp background and some striking titles that exhort to enter the WhatsApp number to hack. They even have boxes to mark what we want to download: texts, videos and/or images.

The process is the same. After entering a mobile phone number, the website starts to “download the hacked data”. Upon reaching 100%, it tells us that the WhatsApp account has been successfully hacked.

Unlike the previous websites, Hackingtor WhatsApp does not show the account data. It directly shows you a button to “download the complete message history”.

As you have probably already realized, spy whatsapp with Hackingtor is not possible. Clicking on the download button will take us to an advertising page.

Remember that it does not matter that these websites have secure HTTPS encryption. Any website can have this SSL certificate, so it does not guarantee that it is a trustworthy site.

WhatsApp Spy Apps

In Google Play there are some applications that claim to be able to spy on WhatsApp for free without a credit card from another mobile and without them noticing.

And although some of these applications indicate that they are a joke, there are others that are not. You can come across apps like Whats Detective (which promises to see your partner’s WhatsApp messages) or WhatsApp Spy (to find out who someone is talking to).

These applications have been and continue to be downloaded by thousands or even millions of people.

But… What do the creators of these apps earn if they are free?

Thanks to advertising and in-app payments, these applications make their creators earn large sums of money. All this taking advantage of the ignorance of users.

This does not happen on iOS devices, because in the Apple Store they are stricter to accept an application in their store. On Android, however, this is very common. Not only because of the deceptive applications that proliferate in the Google store, but also because of the dangerous APKs that are out there on the net.

One of the best known is WhatsApp Sniffer, an app that was born to spy on the conversations of users connected to the same WiFi network, but it was finally removed from Google Play for obvious reasons. Although currently it is still offered as an APK on many sites.

The truth is that these apps, like the websites mentioned above, they are just a hoax.

What do they do then?

Any. They only “bombard” you with advertising and -in the worst case- they try to get you to subscribe to paid services to charge you a certain amount of money every month. Avoid them!

Can WhatsApp be hacked or not?

At this point you may be wondering whether it is possible to hack whatsapp or not.

The truth is that hacking an application like this, with a very high level of security, it is an almost impossible task. Only available to experienced hackers who exploit vulnerabilities, inject malware, or other tactics beyond the knowledge of the average user.

Therefore, believing that a page or application is going to be able to hack anyone’s account in a matter of seconds is absurd.

So stop using programs or pages that promise to spy on someone’s WhatsApp with only their mobile number. ¡It’s not possible!

What is possible, and anyone can do it, is to use the social engineering. That is, take advantage of people’s ignorance to reveal their data.

Not only that, there are also those who convince or take advantage of people’s carelessness to borrow their phone for a few seconds, enough time to get all their WhatsApp conversations for free.

How?

Basically, take the victim’s cell phone and log in to WhatsApp Web to see all their private chats.

whatsapp web﻿

Even if your phone is locked, they can install the WhatsApp app on their phones and log in with your number by verifying by calling your own mobile phone (for which you do not need to unlock it).

As you can see, this can be done by anyone, without having great computer skills. Therefore, be careful who you lend your cell phone to.

In any case, you should know that spying on another person’s WhatsApp conversations (be it a friend, co-worker, partner or family member) is a crime in several countries. Well, it is a violation of privacy and secrecy of communications.

‘Hackers’ who charge to obtain private conversations

It could not miss those who call themselves “hackers” and who offer their services over the Internet or even on the streets themselves.

How to protect myself and avoid falling for these scams

If you still have doubts about whether or not it is possible to hack WhatsApp without a credit card, you should know that no system is 100% secure. Based on this premise, there are vulnerabilities that can be exploited to obtain your data.

One of these is the vulnerability of the SS7 protocol that allows spying on people through the phone. Exploiting this bug, hackers could access your personal accounts that have two-factor authentication activated via SMS (such as Facebook or email).

Therefore, you must be aware and take the necessary security measures to protect your data.

Start by stopping using these pages and apps that, as we have already explained, promise something impossible. It is a matter of common sense: If hacking WhatsApp was as easy and fast as entering the phone number on a web page or application, everyone would be vulnerable to anyone spying on their conversations, right?

You should also be careful who you lend your mobile phone to. If you don’t trust that person, better not let them take your cell phone. Well, in a few seconds they could open a WhatsApp Web session and see your private messages.

Also, you should not install apps to spy WhatsApp on Android or iOS. Well, as we have seen, they do not work. They only serve to display advertisements or, in the worst case, infect your phone with malware.

Finally, rule out the idea of ​​spying on someone’s private communications. The reason? It is a crime that can be punished with jail.
