Lucie decided to get around the law by offering for each NFT “a paper printout of the NFT information.” In a symbolic act of rebellion against the ban, which delayed the sale for a few days, Lucie performed a performance burning the paper impressions, watch the video…

Going into the details of the sale and, according to a note provided by the management of Fauve Paris, 44 NFT works were offered, of which two thirds were sold.
