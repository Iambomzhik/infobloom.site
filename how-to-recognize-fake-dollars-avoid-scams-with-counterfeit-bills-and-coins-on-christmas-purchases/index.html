How to recognize fake dollars? Avoid scams with counterfeit bills and coins on Christmas purchases

ByInfobloom

May 26, 2022

Have you ever been cheated with a counterfeit bill? Tens or thousands of these species circulate in it Ecuador without the authorities being able to calculate its exact amount. The detected They are only those who reach the Finance system national.

When the coins or the fake currency are taken to financial entitiesthese are retained and sent as “presumably false species” to the Central Bank of Ecuador (BCE) for verification.

Once verifiedgo to your final statuswhich can be a currency either counterfeit billdepending on the case.

“The Central Bank carries out a technical review of the species retained by banks and cooperatives. Once analyzed, they respond to the institutions at the national level and indicate the definitive status of the monetary species. If any is false, it is handed over to the Judicial Police to carry out the respective investigations. The ECB does not keep any counterfeit notes, it is only a temporary custodian,” he said. Charles Grandsontechnician responsible for False Species System Of the entity.

the time of elder circulation of coins false in Ecuador is that of increased circulation from cashlike the Christmas season. In this period, the public workers, private, retirees Y food pensioners receive payment from thirteenth loose or Christmas bonus.

From the weekend of Saturday the 14th and Sunday the 15th of December, thousands of people attended the malls, christmas markets Y fairs where they are offered discounts Y offers to make the purchases of this time.

The authorities ask the citizenship check the characteristics of security from coins Y banknotes to avoid being victims of a scam with counterfeit money.

The fake currency that circulate the most in the Ecuador they are the low denomination ones, USD 20, USD 10 and USD 5, since the species of USD 100 or USD 50 are not common in the stores of the country. However, the Central Bank has detected an increase in false species in banknotes of USD 50, which are concentrated in cities borderNieto added.

To more than fake currencythere’s also metallic coins counterfeit that circulate in the Ecuador. The USD 1 (one dollar) and 50 national cents are the most common.

“According to our data, the largest number of counterfeit coins in circulation are one dollar, in gold presentation,” Nieto said. These can be the ones with the face of the North American aboriginal with the child on her back or the commemorative pieces where the portraits of the presidents of USA. According to the official, there are also fake $1 silver coins, but in smaller quantities

some coins fractional 50-cent nationals have also been counterfeit. There are no records of other smaller denomination pieces since it is not a business for counterfeiters. The same goes for the smallest denomination American currency. These cases have not been found because the model of the lower value is more complex and more difficult to falsify, Nieto maintains.

According to the statistics of ECB, Quito is the city with the highest circulation of false species, but it has also been identified in tulcan, Ibarra, Ambato, Santo Domingo, Guayaquil. Every month it fluctuates, the official added.

The technician called on the public to be vigilant so as not to be victims of this kind of scamsespecially in this time of cash purchases and hassles.

“The circulation of counterfeit coins on buses is common. For example, a person gets on the bus, pays, they give him the change, he only limits himself to saying that he is okay. Then they notice and realize that they have given him a fake coin, or some currency from another country, casino chips, old Trolley chips, etc. This is more because of the rush.”

To avoid being victim from fraud with banknotes either fake coinsThe important thing is to take the time to check the money that comes into your hands. There are many ways to detect if a species is not true.

In the banknotespeople should take into account the paper money, which should be consistent. Not smooth or too rough. They should also look at the colourthe sharpness of the microprints and the traits of the characters.

To recognize the fake coinsthe users They must pay attention to the color, the borders, the definition of the characters and the engravings. Nieto details that “American coins have a gold and reddish edge, however, many of these false coins are armed, like a pasted cookie or in turn have a single color, darker, very yellowish or look rusty.”

The official He said that “it goes without saying that if a citizen receives a counterfeit bill or coin, perhaps due to carelessness or an error, he should not look for another person to harm. The best thing that can be done there is to break the circle at that moment and prevent further harm from being caused, and even worse, to the people who work honestly.”

Article 304 of the Comprehensive Criminal Code (COIP) has a sanction for people who circulate a false species with up to three years in prison. The law establishes that “the person who introduces, acquires, commercializes, circulates or circulates adulterated, modified or falsified currency in any way, will be sanctioned with a custodial sentence of one to three years.”

If you are going to do transactions in cashcheck very well the bills and coins you receive so as not to be victims of scams with counterfeit monetary species.

The ECB provides training services, aimed at public entities, private Already general citizenshipsuch as groups, collectives or associations that have a specific need, to learn to identify the false species of the real ones. In these talks, the subject of securities and the care of paper money, banknotes and monetary species that are mistreated are insisted on, Nieto pointed out.
