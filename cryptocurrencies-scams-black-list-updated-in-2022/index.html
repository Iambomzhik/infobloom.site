Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions:

✸ Are online investments reliable?

Of course YES, what is not reliable are all the systems that in El Vigilante we are going to analyze in depth to uncover and deny their legitimacy. Investing in binaries can be a very lucrative business as long as you have the right knowledge that is needed to be able to earn money. On the other hand, you have to stay away from all the cryptocurrency, fx and binary scams that are circulating on the internet.

✸ Are cryptocurrencies safe?

As long as the investments are made from regulated brokers, investments in cryptocurrencies are 100% safe. The problem comes when a user registers and deposits in a broker that is not legitimate, in this case it can happen that the user is scammed and loses all the capital deposited or that he cannot withdraw the profits obtained from the operations carried out.

✸ Are online investments legal?

Online investments are completely legal, as they are regulated by competent bodies such as CySec, the CNMV, etc. What is not legal are the systems that promise millionaire profits in a few days or the software that promises overly optimistic success rates. You have to be very careful because you can fall for one of the many cryptocurrency scams.
